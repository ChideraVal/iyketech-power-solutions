About Us

IykeTech Power Solutions is a dependable power equipment service company in Abuja dedicated to keeping homes, businesses, and facilities running without interruption. We specialize in the repair, maintenance, and sales of generators, ATS panels, generator modules, and injector nozzles, providing practical solutions that help customers restore power quickly and keep their systems performing at their best. From emergency fault tracing and urgent repairs to routine servicing, parts replacement, and technical support, our work is focused on speed, reliability, and lasting results. With a 24/7 service schedule and a commitment to customer satisfaction, IykeTech Power Solutions is the trusted choice for anyone who needs expert power support, quality components, and responsive service they can count on.
